There are 4 effects:
Paralysis
Poison
Burn
Sleep

The Paralysis condition will have the effect of lowering chance of attack to 75% of its usual chance
The Poison condition will inflict damage equal to 1/8 of the targets max hp every turn
Badly Poisoned TBD after creating Poisoned
Burn will work the same as Poisoned
Sleep will disallow attacks until a random number under between 0 and 30 has been chosen.
    The random number will be between 0 and 10
